Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing the School First App (referred to as "we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide
  • Account Information: When you register, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and profile picture and school information.
  • Content and Preferences: Information you share while using the app, such as preferences for news, school events, posts on discussion forums, comments, and participation in webinars.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase courses or event tickets, we collect payment details through secure third-party payment processors.

1.2 Information You Provide
  • Device Information: Device Information: Details about the device you use, including operating system, device type, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Logs of your interactions with the app, such as time spent, pages viewed, and features accessed.
  • Location Data: Approximate location information to provide location-based features like nearby events.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from trusted third-party sources, such as social media platforms if you link your account, or partners hosting webinars or events.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver personalized content, courses, and event recommendations.
  • Facilitate school engagement through games, expert sessions, and webinars.
  • Process payments and provide receipts for purchases.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience through analytics.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, new features, and promotional offers.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activity.

3. Sharing Your Information

3.1 With Service Providers

We share information with vendors and service providers who assist us in app operation, payment processing, and analytics.

3.2 For Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and users’ safety.

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the involved parties.

3.4 With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you explicitly authorize us to do so.

4. Your Choices

Access and Update:

You can update or correct your personal information in your account settings.

Opt-Out:

You can opt out of promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.

Data Deletion:

You can request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us at contact@lifeboat.co.in

5.5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no system is entirely secure. By using our app, you acknowledge and accept these risks.

6. Children's Privacy

School First is designed for use by families, including parents and children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data without consent, please contact us.

7.International Users

If you are accessing School First from outside India, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with this policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or email. Your continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
